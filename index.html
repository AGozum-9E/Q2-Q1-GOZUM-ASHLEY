María Josefa Gabriela
Cariño de Silang
Birthdate March 19, 1731
Died: September 20, 1763
Born in: Caniogan, Santa, Ilocos
Mother: Dominga de los Santos
Father: Anselmo Cariño

  • Female leader of the Ilocano independence movement from Spain.
  • Replaced Diego Silang after his assassination in 1763.
  • Lead for four months before she was captured.


José Protasio Rizal Mercado
y Alonso Realonda
Birthdate June 19, 1861
Died: December 30, 1896
Born in: Calamba, Laguna
Mother: Teodora Alonso Reolonda
Father: Francisco Rizal Mercado

  • Through his writings, he exposed the injustices and corruption of Spanish colonial rule, such as Noli Me Tangere
  • Created Liga Filipina
  • Inspired Filipinos to join the reform movement.


Juan Novicio Luna
Birthdate October 23, 1857
Died: December 7, 1899
Born in: Badoc, Ilocos Norte
Mother: Laureana Novicio y Ancheta
Father: Joaquín Luna de San Pedro y Posadas

  • Filipino painter whose art impacted the country.
  • His paintings showcase all the emotions.
  • One of the first recognized Filipino Artist


Emilio Aguinaldo y Famy
Birthdate March 22, 1869
Died: February 6, 1964
Born in: Kawit, Cavite
Mother: Trinidad Famy y Villanueva
Father: Carlos Aguinaldo y Jamir

  • Elected as the first official President of the Philippines.
  • Achieved Independence


Lapu-Lapu
Birthdate 1491
Died: 1542
Born in: Mactan, Philippines

  • First Filipino Hero
  • Hindered Ferdinand Magellan and his army for their attempt to colonise Mactan Island.
  • Datu/Chief of Mactan
  • Name was first recorded as Çilapulapu
